Location

Few attractions to connect and enjoy with nature.
  • Asud : The famous ancient temple of Shri Keshav and Shri Vyagreshwar.
  • Karde : Virgin Beach.
  • Murud : 18th Century's ancient Durgadevi temple and beach.
  • Harai : Fort, light house and fish auction.
  • Anjarle : Famous ancient Ganpati Temple known as Kadyawarcha Ganpati.
  • Ade : Ancient Shri Parshuram Temple and clean beach.
  • Kelshi : Famous Swayambhu Mahalaxmi temple, Yakub Baba darga and naturally formed sand dune due to Tsunami.
  • Ladghar : Its popular for its beaches and landscape. Tamas Tirth.
  • Dabhol : Chandika Devi temple underground in natural cave. Jetty and power project.
  • Unhavare : Famous for hot springs with faint-smell of sulphur.
  • Panhalekaji : Discovered in 1970 the complex of 28-29 caves.
  • Dapoli : Rich culture and heritage place of Britishers military camp.
